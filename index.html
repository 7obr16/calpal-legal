Privacy Policy

CalPal · Last updated: 2 July 2026

CalPal ("we", "us") is a calorie- and nutrition-tracking app. This policy explains what data the app handles, where it lives, and your choices. We designed CalPal to keep the large majority of your data on your device.

1. Data stored only on your device

The following never leaves your iPhone unless you explicitly enable a sync feature:

  • Onboarding answers and health inputs you provide (e.g. height, weight, goal weight, activity level, dietary preference).
  • Your daily logs: food entries, calories, macros, water, weight, fasting, and any meal photos you capture (stored locally on the device).
  • App settings and your subscription state cache.

This data is held in your device's local app storage and is removed when you delete the app or delete your account (see section 6).

2. Meal analysis (photos & descriptions)

When you scan a meal or type a description, that image or text is sent to our processing backend, which forwards it to our AI provider (OpenAI) solely to estimate the food and its calories, and returns the result to your device. This content is used only to produce your estimate — it is not stored on our servers, not logged, and not used to train models or to advertise to you.

3. Account information

If you create an account with Sign in with Apple, we receive a unique Apple user identifier and, only on your first sign-in, the name and email (which may be Apple's private-relay email) you choose to share. We use this only to identify your account and, if enabled, to sync your data across your devices. We do not sell this information.

4. Purchases & subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat, which provides us with purchase and entitlement status (not your payment details) so we can unlock premium features and restore purchases. Apple handles all billing.

5. What we do NOT do

  • No third-party advertising or ad tracking SDKs.
  • No selling or renting of your personal data.
  • No cross-app or cross-site tracking of you.

6. Deleting your data & account

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account in the app. This removes your local data on the device, deletes your account record on our side, and revokes CalPal's Sign in with Apple token. Deleting the app also removes all on-device data.

7. Children

CalPal is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

8. Data retention

On-device data persists until you remove it or delete the app. Account records are retained until you delete your account. Meal photos/descriptions sent for analysis are processed transiently and not retained.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email emilarnoldmedia@gmail.com.

This policy may be updated as the app evolves; the "Last updated" date reflects the latest revision.
