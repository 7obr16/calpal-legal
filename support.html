Frequently asked questions

How does the AI food scan work?
Take a photo of your meal — CalPal analyzes it and estimates calories, protein, carbs and fat. Estimates are approximations; you can always adjust portions or log manually.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are handled by Apple: open Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

How do I restore a purchase?
Open CalPal → tap the gear on the home screen → Restore Purchases.

How do I delete my account?
Open CalPal → gear → Delete Account. This removes your account and revokes your Sign in with Apple link.

Is my data shared or sold?
No. See our Privacy Policy for details.
